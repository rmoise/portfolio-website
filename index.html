About Me

Hailing from the vibrant streets of Brooklyn by way of Queens, my design journey began amidst the creative energy of NYC's bustling ad agencies balancing both digital and print work. My passion for modernist design, especially the Bauhaus movement, drew me to Berlin, where I've continued to explore UI design and, more recently, UX and web development. I’m driven by a desire to create clean, user-centered experiences that marry form and function.
